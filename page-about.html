About Us

ABOUT US

We are Midrand Association for Sports and Arts, a representative of Athletes, Artists and Administrators within the sector in Johannesburg, Midrand South Africa.

Mission & Vision

To promote sports and encourage participation in our communities from young to old
Protect players, Artists & Administrators, promote and encourage fair play.
To promote health, wellness and social cohesion.
To be a trusted and efficient umbrella body in Arts and Sport in Midrand and surrounding areas.

Aims & Objectives


To promote activities and participation in Sports & Arts.
No age or gender preferences.
To promote multiple sporting codes.
To work with like minded organizations like ours.
